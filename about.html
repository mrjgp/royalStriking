Head Coach Aaron Meisner is a former professional mixed martial artist and Muay Thai competitor, having also spent the last 13 years as a fight coach and personal trainer.  Throughout his career he has always strived to grow and evolve as both a coach and individual, and brings that energy into teaching others at Royal Striking.

​

As a young and inexperienced amateur fighter, he worked hard, took chances, kept an open mind, and ultimately discovered his passion.  This journey around the world brought Aaron to start crafting the vision for Royal.  His diverse experience has allowed him to create an environment where people of all backgrounds can learn, grow, and have fun.
